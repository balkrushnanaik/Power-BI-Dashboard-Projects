Here are some realistic **business questions** you can answer using your UpGrad Learning Support Center dataset in Power BI:

### Enrollment Analysis

1. How many students enrolled each month?
2. Which course has the highest number of enrollments?
3. Which city contributes the most enrollments?
4. What is the enrollment trend over time?
5. Which counselor enrolled the most students?

### Revenue Analysis

6. What is the total revenue generated from course fees?
7. How much revenue comes from each course?
8. What is the revenue contribution by city?
9. How much revenue is pending due to unpaid or partially paid fees?
10. Which course generates the highest average fee per student?

### Lead Source Analysis

11. Which lead source generates the most enrollments?
12. Which lead source generates the highest revenue?
13. What is the conversion rate of each lead source?
14. Which lead source produces the most placed students?
15. What percentage of students come from referrals?

### Student Performance Dashboard

16. What is the average attendance percentage by course?
17. Which course has the highest average final test score?
18. Is there a relationship between attendance and final test scores?
19. How many students scored above 80 in the final test?
20. Which trainer's students have the highest average scores?

### Placement Dashboard

21. What is the overall placement rate?
22. Which course has the highest placement rate?
23. Which company hired the most students?
24. What is the average placement package by course?
25. Which city has the highest number of placed students?
26. What percentage of completed students got placed?
27. What is the distribution of placement packages?

### Trainer Performance Dashboard

28. Which trainer handles the most students?
29. Which trainer has the highest average attendance rate?
30. Which trainer has the highest average assignment score?
31. Which trainer has the highest placement rate?
32. Which trainer's students report the highest satisfaction ratings?

### Student Satisfaction Dashboard

33. What is the average student satisfaction rating?
34. Which course receives the highest satisfaction rating?
35. Is satisfaction related to placement status?
36. Which trainer receives the highest student ratings?
37. How does satisfaction vary across cities?

### Advanced Business Questions

38. Do students with attendance above 85% perform better in final tests?
39. Does payment status affect course completion rates?
40. Which combination of course and lead source generates the highest revenue?
41. Which counselors bring the highest-value students?
42. What factors most influence placement success?
43. What is the average package for students scoring above 80?
44. Which course offers the best ROI (Placement Package vs Course Fee)?
45. What percentage of students are still ongoing?
46. Which batch has the highest completion rate?
47. Which batch has the highest placement rate?
48. What are the top 5 insights management should focus on?

These questions are excellent for creating Power BI dashboards, KPIs, drill-through pages, slicers, and DAX measures.
